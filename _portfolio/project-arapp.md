'Greet Flood' is a mobile app with enhanced interactivity for demonstrating natural and nature-based solutions for flood management. NBS is much less popular among the public due to minimal awareness and available examples, resulting in a lack of understanding and admiration among the public. To bridge this knowledge gap, the 'Greet Flood' app is being developed with access to information from many demonstration sites around the world, featuring virtual video tours and augmented reality-based live tours.

A prototype project based on Diyasaru Park, an urban wetland located in Colombo Sri Lanka was completed from June 2022 - June 2023. It was presented at the WWF Youth Champions Programme in June 2023 conducted by the environmental and disaster management unit of World Wildlife Fund.

The next phase of the app is currently being developed.
